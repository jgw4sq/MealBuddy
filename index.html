The Details

How Meal Buddy Works

Buy/Sell unused meal swipes and plus dollars that would have gone to waste

Pick your Food

Meal Buddy provides a full live menu of dining halls and other on-grounds dining locations to make your meal customizable.

Make an Offer

Make an initial offer to either buy or provide a meal from an on grounds dining location.

Negotiate the Price

Both buyers and sellers can easily request their desired price, and easily make a counteroffer if the price isn't right.

Wait for your Food

Meal Buddy provides real-time wait estimates while the seller picks up your customized order.

Meet at Specified Location

Meet at the agreed meeting spot, and enjoy your meal or extra spending money!

Rate your Meal Buddy

Give your meal buddy a rating based on your order and transaction satisfaction.
