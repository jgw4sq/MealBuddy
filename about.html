What do we do?

Meal Buddy provides students the opportunity to buy food from dining halls and other on grounds eating locations at a discount using their friends' extra meal swipes and plus dollars.

Why Use Meal Buddy?

  • Buy food at a discount while remaining on grounds!
  • Make use of your swipes and plus dollars that would have otherwise gone to waste!
  • Negotiate your own prices and avoid waiting in long lines for your food!

What do we stand for?

  • Allow students to make full use of their unlimited meal plan that they are paying for.
  • Allow students to get a convenient, healthy meal without breaking the bank!

Buy food from a variety of locations at a inexpensive price. Get food conveniently and on grounds. Make use of your unused swipes. Earn spending money that every college kid needs!
